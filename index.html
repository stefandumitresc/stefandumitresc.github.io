Hey, I'm Stefan!

I'm a 21-year-old senior from Seattle, WA at UW-Madison where I'm pursuing Computer Science with a Certificate in Economic Analytics. I believe technology should solve real problems, not create new ones.

My journey started with a curiosity about how systems work at scale.
